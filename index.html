Marry My Husband

My favorite K-Drama

An extraordinary revenge drama begins to pay back the husband and friend who betrayed her! Can there be a life as miserable as this? Kang Ji-won witnesses an affair between her husband and best friend and is also diagnosed with terminal cancer. Not even living out her limited time, she gets murdered. However, perhaps even fate pitied her, as Kang Ji-won's life takes an unexpected turn. She returns ten years with all her memories, beginning her second chance at life. She meticulously plans her revenge against her husband and friend to avoid the same betrayal. Her revenge will continue until everything is set right again. A satisfying revenge against voracious desires and betrayal begins.
